Welcome to The Vegetable Battle!

The rules:

  1. Each player plays in turn;
  2. For each turn, a player can move from one to three boxes (horizontally or vertically) before ending their turn.
  3. If a player passes over a box containing a weapon, they leave their current weapon on site and replace it with the new one.
  4. If players cross over adjacent squares (horizontally or vertically), a battle begins!
  5. The player can choose to attack or defend against the next shot.
  6. The damage depends on the player's weapon:
    • The default weapon inflicts 10 points of damage,
    • The corn inflicts 20 points of damage,
    • The cabbage inflicts 30 points of damage,
    • The onion inflicts 40 points of damage,
    • The tomato inflicts 50 points of damage;
  7. If the player chooses to defend themselves, they sustain 50% less damage than normal.
  8. As soon as the life points of a player (initially 100) falls to 0, they lose.
